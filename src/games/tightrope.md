Scoring cards 0-10 (treat 10 as blue 0); Tightrope Cards 11-60; 25 red tokens, 25 blue tokens
#### Aim of the Game
In this game, both the winner and loser of each trick get points. Points of each color cancel out the other color. The goal is to end a round (9 tricks) with as few points as possible.
#### Preparation
* Shuffle the scoring cards and place them face down in the middle.
* Place the tokens in the middle.
* Shuffle all the tightrope cards, then deal 9 cards to each player.
* Put aside the remaining cards until the next round.
#### Playing the Game
* The player to the left of the dealer begins by revealing the top scoring card. When a scoring card with value 0 is turned up, the player immediately reveals a second scoring card.  The 0 scoring card is then used to cover the value of the corresponding color on the second scoring card.  This means that tokens of only one color will be distributed this trick. If both 0 scoring cards are turned up one after another, they are discarded and play continues normally with the next scoring card in the deck.
* The player then plays one of his tightrope cards face up in front of him.  In clockwise order, each player likewise plays a tightrope card face up in front of them.  This ends the trick.
* The player who played the highest card takes as many blue tokens as indicated on the scoring card.  The player who played the lowest card takes as many red tokens as indicated on the scoring card.  All tokens should be visible to all.
* After the trick is concluded, all involved tightrope cards are discarded.  The player who played the highest card begins the next trick by revealing the next scoring card and playing a tightrope card.
* All revealed scoring cards remain face up so players can keep in mind which scoring cards are yet to come.
* As soon as a player possesses tokens of both colors, he returns pairs of one red and one blue token until he has only one color remaining.  The remaining tokens indicate how much he is out of balance.
#### Scoring
* A round is over when all 9 tricks have been concluded.  Then, for each player, the number of his remaining tokens is recorded, ignoring color – the fewer the better.
* If a player succeeds in finishing the round in perfect balance (that is, with no tokens), he may delete his highest score from one of the previous rounds.
#### The Next Round
* All balancing tokens are returned to the middle after end-of-round scoring.
* The next player in clockwise order becomes the dealer, and play continues as before.
#### Game End
* After the appropriate number of rounds (as many as there are players), the game concludes.
* The player with the lowest total wins!
#### Variants
* For a more tactical game, play with the exact number of cards needed for the participating players.
* For a less tactical game, play cards simultaneously instead of in clockwise order.
