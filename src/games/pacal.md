Cards 1-50

#### **Setup**
* Shuffle the 50 cards and give ten face down to each player. With these, each makes a pyramid of 10 cards in front of them. This pyramid is the base on which the other cards will be placed facing up.  
* From the other 30 cards left each player takes 15\.

#### **Game Process**
* The game is played in rounds. For each round the players have to build their pyramids. The first one who wins 3 rounds wins the game.  
* Each player shows the card from the top of his pyramid and then turns it down again and the one who shows the higher value starts.  
* The start player shops 2 cards from his hand by placing them on the table face up.  
* The partner picks one card and the player gets the other.  
* Now the card that each player received must be placed on one of the 10 fields of his pyramid.  
* Now the other player plays two cards, and that’s the way the game goes on changing turns each time.  
* After a player has played 14 cards, he can use the last card in his hand on his own pyramid if possible.

#### **Building Rules**
* The cards must be ordered so that the highest value is on the top of the pyramid. All the other values must be ordered from left to right. On the base of the pyramid the card on the right must have the lowest value.  
* Once all the cards are placed they can’t be moved again.  
* Each card value can be covered by a second one, but each card can only be covered once

#### **The End of a Round**
* As soon as one of the players finishes his pyramid (meaning that all 10 fields are full), the round ends. This player is the winner.  
* If both players finish their pyramids at the same time, then the winner will be the one that has the higher difference between the lowest and the highest card value.  
* If at any point a player can not place his card on his fields as the building rules said because he has to cover the cards again, he immediately loses the game.  
* Play until one player has won three rounds, shuffling the complete deck and starting over for each round.

#### **Solo Game**
* All cards are shuffled. A pyramid is formed of 10 cards face down. The remaining 40 cards form a drawpile.  
* On each turn, take the top card from the drawpile and immediately try to build it into the pyramid. The same building rules as above apply.  
* The game is over and won when the pyramid is completed (each field is occupied). Count the number of cards used to see how well you did. The best possible result is 10 cards.  
* If a card no longer fits in the pyramid, you have lost \- but feel free to try again.
