Cards 1-100

## Goal

Play cards into rows without being forced to take cards.  
Each card shows points.  These are **points you don’t want**.  
After 2 rounds, the player with the **fewest points** wins.

## Setup

1. Shuffle the number cards and place **one face‑up card** in each of 3 rows.  
2. Deal **8 number cards** to each player.  
3. Return unused cards to the box.

## How to Play

### 1\. Play a Card

Each turn:

* Everyone secretly chooses a card.  
* Reveal simultaneously.  
* Cards are played in **ascending order** (lowest card goes first).

Place your card to the right of a row according to these rules:

### Rule 1: Ascending Order

Your card must be higher than the last card in that row.

### Rule 2: Smallest Difference

Your card must go to the row where the difference between your card  
and that row’s last card is the smallest.

### 2\. Taking Cards

You must take cards if:

### Rule 3: The Row is Full

A row fills when it reaches its limit:

* 3→ row: full at 3 cards  
* 4→ row: full at 4 cards  
* 5→ row: full at 5 cards

If your card *completes* a row:

* Take **all existing cards** in that row.  
* Your played card becomes the **new first card** of that row.

### Rule 4: Your Card is Too Low

If your card is lower than all rows’ last cards:

* You choose **one row** to take.  
* Your card becomes that row’s new first card.

## What to Do With Taken Cards

When you take cards:

1. Pick **exactly one card** to add to your **X row**.  
2. All other taken cards go to your **hand**, and you may play them later.

If you took only one card, it must be placed in your X row.

## X Row and X Pile

* Place cards in your X row in **ascending order**.  
* If a new card does **not** fit the ascending order:  
  * Move your entire X row face‑down into your **X pile**.  
  * Start a new X row with that card.

Cards in your **X pile** score double points at the end of the round.

## End of the Round

A round ends immediately when any player has **no cards left in hand**.

Score:

* Each point on cards in your **hand** \= **1 point**  
* Each point in your **X pile** \= **2 points**  
* Cards in your X row do **not** score

Record everyone's score, reset the game, and begin the next round.

## End of the Game

After **two rounds**, add the scores.  
The player with the **fewest total points** wins\!
