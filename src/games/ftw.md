Cards 1-60; 40 Minutes;

### Goal:
End a round with **1 high card**. You score that card's value **minus** all other cards in your hand.

### Setup

* Use cards:
  * 2-3 players: **1-36**
  * 4 players: **1-48**
  * 5-6 players: **1-60**
* Deal:
  * 2 players: **12 each**, 12 get discarded aside
  * 3-5 players: **12 each**
  * 6 players: **10 each**
* Everyone **passes 3 cards to the right**.
* Play **one round per player**.

### Turn (play exactly 1 card)

A shared **face-up discard pile** is in the center.

**A) Play Higher**
Play a card **higher than the top** of the discard pile
(or any card if the pile is empty).

**B) Play Lower in Front of You**
Play a **lower** card face-up in front of you (an _auxiliary_ card).
Then **take 1 visible card** from the discard pile into your hand.
Discard the rest of the pile. Next player starts a new pile.

**C) Use Auxiliary Cards**
Add the value of **one or more auxiliary cards** to a card in your hand.
If the **sum beats the discard top**, play that lower card.
Used auxiliary cards are discarded and gone.

### End of Round & Scoring

* The round ends when a player finishes a turn with **1 card in hand**.
* Score:
  **Highest card** in hand
  **minus** all other cards in hand
  (auxiliary cards don't count; minimum score = 0).

### Win

After all rounds, **highest total score wins**.
