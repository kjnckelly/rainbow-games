Values of each cards A-10 are worth 1-10
J is worth 11, K and Q are worth 0

**Setup**: Deal 3/4/5 players 13/13/10 Cards. (At 5 leave extras aside).

**Trick**: Dealer Leads, each player, in clockwise order, plays 1 card.
- They must follow suit. If they can’t, they may play any card.
- And they then state the sum of the values of the cards played so far.
- If a player equals or surpasses the limit (14/16/17 at 3/4/5 players),
they immediately win the trick and capture the trick.
- If the limit is not equaled or surpassed after everyone has played, the
highest card of the lead suit captures the trick.
- If there’s a tie, tied player who placed first wins the trick.
- Winner sorts captured cards by suit, with their values visible.
- Winner leads the next trick.

**End of a Hand**
- When a player empties their hand the remaining players finish the rest of
the trick and then the hand ends.
- At the end of the hand, players with cards remaining may play any number
of cards from their hand face-down, then reveal them simultaneously and
add them to their captured cards.

**Scoring**:
- For each suit: The player with the most cards in that suit scores 1pt for
each card they captured of that suit.
- Every other player loses 1pt per card in that suit.
- If there’s a tie for most, all players, including those tied, lose points

**New Round**: Player who scored the most this round chooses the next dealer.
- They may choose themselves.
- Tie: tied player earliest in clockwise order from the dealer decides.

**End of the Game**: Play one round per player.
- Player with the highest total score wins.
