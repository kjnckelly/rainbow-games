Cards 1-100; 111-120 as Bull cards
#### Goal:
Be the first player to get rid of all your cards. Taking cards is bad.

#### Setup:
Each player gets **10 cards**, sorted in order. One card starts the first discard pile. The youngest player goes first.

**On Your Turn, Do One Thing:**
##### Option 1: Play a Card
Play **one card** onto any discard pile:

* It must be **higher** than the top card.
* It must be **within 10** of that card.
* Numbers **loop after 100** - so after 100 comes 1, 2, etc.

##### Option 2: Take a Pile
If you can't play - or don't want to - take **one entire discard pile** into your hand.

* When you take a pile, flip **two new cards** to start **two new discard piles**.
* If the pile you take has **3 or more cards**, you gain a **Bull Card**.

#### Bull Cards:
Bull Cards let you break the normal limit:

* Each Bull Card lets you play **multiple cards** on a pile in one turn, as long as they all fit the "within 10" rule.
* With more Bull Cards, you can do this on multiple piles.

#### End of a Round:
The round ends immediately when someone runs out of cards.

* Everyone else counts the **bullhead icons** on their remaining cards as **minus points**.
* Play as many rounds as players.
* **Lowest total score wins.**
