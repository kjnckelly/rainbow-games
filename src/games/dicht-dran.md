Cards 1-100

This is *Dicht Dran*. The goal is to **get rid of all your cards and score the most chiles**.

Each card is worth chiles:
**Multiples of 5 are worth 3**, **multiples of 3 but not 5 are worth 2**, and **everything else is worth 1**.

To set up, **everyone gets 8 cards**. The **1 and the 100** go face up in the middle, and **four more random cards** are placed face up in a circle between them.

Each round has a **target card** and a **safe zone**.
Starting from the **1**, the next card clockwise is the **target**. The **two cards next to it** define the **safe zone**-any number between them is safe.

Everyone secretly plays **one card face down**, then we reveal.
The card **closest to the target** wins the round-**ties go to the higher number**.

The winner:

* **Scores chiles equal to the target card**
* **Moves their played card into the center**

Everyone else discards their card:

* If your card was **inside the safe zone**, nothing happens
* If it was **outside the safe zone**, you **draw cards equal to its chile value**

Then the **next card clockwise** becomes the new target and we repeat.

The game ends when someone has **no cards and doesn't have to draw**.
Add the chiles you earned and **subtract chiles from cards left in hand**.
We usually play **two games and add the scores**.

That's it.
