Cards 1-98; 6 blue \+ 6 red chips

## Goal
Score the most points by collecting positive points and avoiding negative ones. When you place the 5th card in a row, you must take that entire row \- good and bad.

## Setup
* Give each player **1 red chip (x2)** and **1 blue chip (+5)**  
* Shuffle cards  
* Deal cards per player:  
  * 2p: 14 each / 7 on table  
  * 3p: 12 each / 9 on table  
  * 4p: 12 each / 12 on table  
  * 5p: 12 each / 15 on table  
  * 6p: 10 each / 15 on table  
* Sort table cards in ascending order clockwise  
* Each table card starts a row

## Turn Structure
On your turn, take 1-3 actions.

Actions:

* play a card  
* play a red x2 chip  
* play a blue \+5 chip

Turns proceed clockwise.

## Placing Cards
A card must go in the **only row it fits**:

* Higher than that row's last number  
* Lower than the next row's last number (clockwise)

Exception: If your number is higher than all rows or lower than all rows, place it in the row with the **highest number**.

Cards extend rows outward.

## Taking a Row
If you place the **5th card** in a row:

* Immediately take the entire row  
* Include chips from that row  
* Stack face down in front of you

You can take multiple rows in one turn.

## Chips
* Placed inward under the row  
* Each row may hold:  
  * 1 \+5 blue chip  
  * 1 x2 red chip

Effects at scoring:

* \+5 card \= \+5 points  
* x2 doubles your total  
* multiple x2 stack (quadruple, etc.)

Penalty: Unplayed chips \= **\-10 each**

## End of Game
Game ends when all cards are played.

Players with no playable chips or cards stop acting.

Leftover rows do not score.

## Scoring
Card values for rainbow deck: Normal cards are \+1 points. All cards ending in 2 (including 22\) are worth \+2. Cards with other values are negative whatever they say.

1. Add positive points  
2. Subtract negative points  
3. Add \+5 bonuses  
4. Multiply by x2  
5. Subtract \-10 per unused chip

Highest score wins.
