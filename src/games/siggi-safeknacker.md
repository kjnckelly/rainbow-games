Cards 1-4,11-62; One Dice;5 chips.

### Objective
Earn and keep the most loot by correctly guessing the next card for the safes.

### Setup
Spread the 1 - 4 cards out on the table.  These will be "safes" or placeholders for the cards to come.
Shuffle the remaining cards.
Put one card face up next to each safe.
Place the remaining cards face down as a draw pile.

### Turn
#### Option 1: Attempt to Open a Safe
A player will roll the dice.  On a 1 - 4, the player will be trying to "open" the safe with the matching number.
On a 5 or 6, the player can choose which safe they want to try to open.
The player will look at the current (top) card in that safe, and predict if the next card from the draw deck will be **higher or lower** than the current card.   
If correct, the player has successfully opened the safe, and gets to take the current card, and any cards beneath it. The new card will replace it in the safe.
If incorrect, the player has to lose any unsecured cards in their loot pile, and place them in the safe.  The new card will go on top in this safe.
#### Option 2: Stash the loot.
A player can move all cards from their loot pile to their stash pile.  Place a chip on top of this pile to indicate that this loot is secured.

### End of Game.
Take turns in order until the draw pile is empty.  The winner is the one with the most loot (both stashed and unsecured).
