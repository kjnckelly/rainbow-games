Cards 2-111

### OBJECTIVE
Score the most points by collecting sets of goods (Color sets where the first digit matches) — there are 9 cards (X1-X9) in each color set.

### SETUP
Remove the X0 cards (which we will call SCORE cards) from the deck, shuffle the deck including the 2x and wild cards, and deal **4 cards** to each player.  
Shuffle the 10 SCORE cards back into the deck and shuffle again.  
Reveal the top **3 cards** and place them face up next to the deck.

### TURN
Each player has **3 Actions** per turn. They may choose from the following 6 actions (the same action may be repeated as long as you have actions):

##### Play a Card
* Play a card from your hand, then place it **face down** in front of you to start a **goods pile**. This is the starting card of that pile.
* Play a card from your hand face up onto an existing goods pile so that **all cards remain visible**. Note if this is the first face-up good, then the **fox moves to this pile**.

##### Take a Card
* Take one of the 3 face-up cards and immediately replace it with the top card from the deck.
* Draw a card from the deck. **Costs two Actions**

##### Move the Fox
If the fox is on one of your stacks:
* Move the fox onto another of your stacks.
* Move the fox to your left hand neighbor.  **Costs two Actions**

### GOODS PILES
- Any card may be placed face down as the **starting card** of a pile.  
- Each pile may only contain **one type of good**, but it does *not* have to match the starting concealed card.  
- Each player may have a maximum of **10 piles** (one for each type of good).  
- Piles may also contain wild cards and **one** x2 card.  
- Players may only place cards on **their own piles**.  
- Starting face-down cards may never be looked at again, not even by their owner.


### SPECIAL CARDS
**Wild Cards: 111,113,115,117,119**  
May be added to a pile, but never as the first face-up card.

**x2 Cards: 112,114,116,118,120**  
May be added to any pile. Once added, no more cards may be added to that pile.  
The pile’s score is doubled, but the x2 card does **not** count toward pile size.

### SCORE CARDS (over 100)
- When a SCORE card appears, set it aside and replace it with another card.  
- When the **4th, 7th, and 10th** SCORE cards appear, scoring occurs.  
- If the 1st or 2nd scoring happens in the middle of a turn, the player finishes their turn after scoring.  
- The game ends immediately after the **third** scoring.


### SCORING
- The collection with the fox on it does not score any points.

#### UNIQUE
- If a player is the **only** one who has a certain type of good, they receive **4 points**.

#### FIRST PLACE
- The player with the **most** of a good type receives **3 points**.  
- If two players tie for first, each receives **2 points**.  
- If three or more players tie, each receives **1 point**.

#### SECOND PLACE
- There is **no second place** if there is a tie for first.  
- Otherwise, the player with the second most receives **1 point**.  
- Ties for second receive **0 points**.

#### END OF GAME
Only during the **third scoring**, players lose:

- **2 points** for each pile that contains only its starting face-down card.  
- **1 point** for each card left in hand.

The player with the most points wins.


## VARIANTS

### Official
To balance turn-order advantage:
- The starting player begins with **0 points**.
- Players 2 / 3 / 4 / 5 begin with **1 / 2 / 3 / 4 points** respectively.

### Unofficial
Each player begins with a **2x card** in front of them.  
On their turn, they may purchase it for **2 AP** and place it into their hand.
