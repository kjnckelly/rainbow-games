Cards 4-13 and 16.

Point values:
* 4 = 1 point
* 5-7 = 2 points
* 3-13 = 3 points
* 16 = 4 points

#### Aim of the Game
Score as many points as possible.

#### Setup
Shuffle the cards and deal five face down to each player.
The 11th card is placed face up next to the play area and is not used during the game.

#### How to play
Each player secretly chooses one card and places it face down in front of them.
Both cards are then revealed at the same time.
The card with the higher value wins the trick — **except** if its value is more than double that of the lower card.
In that case, the lower card wins the trick.

For example:

If one player plays a 10 and the other a 5, the 10 wins.

But if they play a 10 and a 4, the 4 wins.

The winner of the trick places their card face up in front of them.
The loser takes their card back into their hand and may play it again later.
The game continues until one player wins a trick with their second-to-last card, leaving them with only one card left in hand.

#### Scoring
Each player counts the number of points for the cards they have in front of them (the cards with which they won tricks).
Cards remaining in hand do not count.

#### Rematch
Players swap hands, so that each now has the cards their opponent had in the previous round.
A second round is then played in the same way.

#### Victory
Each player adds up their scores from both rounds.
The player with the highest total wins.
